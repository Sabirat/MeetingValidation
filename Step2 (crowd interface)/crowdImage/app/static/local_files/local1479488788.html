﻿﻿Most Alcoholics Anonymous meetings are Open Discussion; which means that anyone is welcome to attend.
Some are Specialized or Closed Meetings. The meeting type will be indicated as such by one or more of
the following abbreviations at the end of the address. No abbreviation indicates an Open Meeting.
ASL-American Sign Language Meeting   ABSI - As Bill Sees it    B-Beginners   BB-Big Book   C-Closed Meeting (for Alcoholics Only) 
D-Discussion   GV - Grapevine  H - History   HC - Handicap Accessible    L - Literature    LS-Living Sober   M-Men Only
