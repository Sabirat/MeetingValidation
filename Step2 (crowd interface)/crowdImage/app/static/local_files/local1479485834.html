Seattle Intergroup Meeting Directory

To obtain a list of meetings you can select which part of the city you are interested in and/or the day of the week. If you select the day and leave the city location at the default you will get a list of every meeting in the city for that day. If you select the city location and leave the day of the week at the default you will get a list of every meeting for the entire week in that part of Greater Seattle.

That said, it will probably be most useful if you select both the day of the week and the city location. It is also possible to filter for "one-hour," "men-only" or "women-only" meetings.
